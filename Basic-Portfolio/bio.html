About Me

In my past work, I have experience with Active Directory, Exchange Server, SharePoint, mac environments, mobile phones, printers, and helpdesk troubleshooting. I've installed Cisco IP Phone systems in offices around the state of Ohio for the Ohio Education Association. My greatest joy comes from the commitment of raising my son. I like to sew clothes, paint with oil, and bake cakes for stress relief. I love everything to do with astrophysics and NASA. Random things to know about me are 1) I've golfed a hole in one on the 18th hole at Armco Park, Ohio, and 2) I taught ballroom dancing a long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away.
